### About me

I'm **Aizharkyn Zhumabaeva** — *Aijar* — a data analyst with an **economics background** and **hands-on e-commerce operations experience**. I spent 8 months running product catalogs on **Shopify** and **Trendyol**, and building stores on **IKAS**, before moving deeper into the analytics side of the business.

That gives me an unusual angle: I know how e-commerce data is *generated* — SKUs, attributes, categories, content quality — not just how to query it. Now I'm sharpening the analytical and ML stack on top of that domain knowledge, with a growing focus on **agentic AI** and open-source LLM tooling.

Based in **Istanbul, Turkey** 🇹🇷 — open to remote roles globally.

### Featured work

> Pinned repositories (set up to four — see checklist below):

🔮 **[sales-forecasting-prophet](https://github.com/Aijarkynhref/sales-forecasting-prophet)** — Time-series forecasting of e-commerce sales using Meta's Prophet, with seasonality decomposition and back-testing.

📊 **[ecommerce-analytics-case](https://github.com/Aijarkynhref/ecommerce-analytics-case)** — End-to-end analytics on a public marketplace dataset: cohort retention, RFM segmentation, unit economics, A/B-test simulation.

🛒 **[catalog-quality-analysis](https://github.com/Aijarkynhref/catalog-quality-analysis)** — Drawing on real Trendyol/Shopify ops experience: how product attribute completeness and content quality correlate with listing performance. Includes an ML classifier that auto-categorizes products from title + description.

🤖 **[cv-tailoring-agent](https://github.com/Aijarkynhref/cv-tailoring-agent)** — A Telegram bot that scrapes job-listing channels, scores vacancy relevance with an LLM, and tailors a CV per opening. My playground for agentic AI patterns.
